Step 1: Select an existing Schedule or generate a new one.

Click Generate Schedule to create a new Schedule. Input a Schedule Name, and we will generate a new unique Schedule ID.
Click Edit Schedule to add classes to an existing Schedule. Input the Schedule ID corresponding to the Schedule you want to edit. (This can be found on the View Schedules page.)

Step 2: Select Schedule Requirememnts

Select a Major Requirement to display all the classes that fulfill it.

Step 3: Choose what classes you want to add to your schedule. Click on the CRN to add a class.
